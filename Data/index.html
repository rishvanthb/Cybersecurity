Data is Everywhere!

Data is everywhere. From your bank account to your email and password, data is ubiquitous. As you know, there are primarily two main types of data. There is qualitative and quantative data. Quantative data can be described numerically. For example, if the CONSOLE prints out "Mandlebrot Set has a magnitude of 15 degree celsius", that data would be quantative. Although its meaning is very questionable. Qualatative data refers to items that cannot be expressed numerically. For example, consider the taste of a lemon. We can say that it is sour. This is a feeling that cannot be measured objectively. The abstract idea is that you cannot quantify it. Today Data is becoming increasingly valuable, so it is important to safeguard it. That's why learning about cybersecurity is very important!
